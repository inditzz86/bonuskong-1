    One round from take-off to vanish

    Every round follows the same loop. A short betting window opens and players place a stake; the plane then lifts off and the multiplier starts climbing from 1.00x. At a point fixed by the random number generator before take-off, the plane leaves the screen and the round ends. Anyone who cashed out earlier keeps stake times the multiplier shown at that moment; anyone still in the round loses the stake.
